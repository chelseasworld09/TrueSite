TERISA RUE

Terisa Marie Tubbs, who performs under the alias of Terisa Rue. (True) is the all American R&B/Pop recording artist, musician, model and actress. The starlet was born and raised in Youngstown, Ohio, and at age 8, she picked up her first self taught instrument; the guitar. Soon after, she learned drums, violin, flute, trumpet, and lastly the keyboard. At age 10, she had her first try at the organized music world and joined a group that consisted of her sister and another young female.
